FAQ

How long before I can use the tub?
Typically, the surface is ready for use within 24 hours.
Is reglazing safe and low-odor?
Yes. We use modern, low-VOC materials and professional ventilation.
How long does it last?
If you follow the maintenance instructions provided after the job is complete, our professional coating can last 5–10 years. We use only commercial-grade materials designed for durability and daily use.
Do you repair chips or rust?
Yes — local repairs are part of prep before refinishing.
What does the warranty cover?
Our warranty covers any peeling, bubbling, or workmanship defects that occur during the warranty period — as long as maintenance instructions are followed. If any issue appears, our technician will visit and fix it free of charge under warranty. Damage caused by dropping heavy objects, improper cleaning products, or failure to follow maintenance guidelines is not covered. If the issue is non-warranty related, we can still send a technician — you'll only pay a $150 service call fee.
Will the bath look brand new after resurfacing?
The resurfacing process gives your bath a fresh, rejuvenated appearance and extends its life by many years. While it's not a factory finish, the results are highly effective, cost-efficient, and visually impressive. For cosmetic repairs – we carefully colour match and blend repairs to ensure a seamless look.
How can I get a quote or schedule a service?
Simply fill out our Instant Estimate form or call/text us your project details. You'll receive an instant quote and a manager will contact you to confirm your appointment time and quote. Your final quote will confirm our manager after we'll receive a photos of the surface.
