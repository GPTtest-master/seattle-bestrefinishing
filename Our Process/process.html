How Our Refinishing Process Works

STEP 1

Get Your Quote & Schedule Appointment

Fill out our instant estimate form or give us a call. We'll provide you with a transparent, no-obligation quote for your refinishing project.

What to expect: Our team will schedule a convenient time that works for you. We respect your schedule and always arrive on time, fully equipped with professional-grade materials.

STEP 2

Preparing for Your Bath's Transformation

Before our technician arrives, we kindly ask you to clear the bathroom of personal items like towels, shampoo, or even the toothbrushes. If you have a shower screen or plan to change the taps, please do so before the work begins.

What to expect from us: Our skilled technician will arrive in our professional uniform, assess the space, and prepare for ventilation. They'll begin the comprehensive refinishing process that's guaranteed to transform your bathroom.

STEP 3

Expert Surface Preparation

We thoroughly clean and prepare your surface, removing all soap scum, oils, and old coatings. Any chips, cracks, or imperfections are carefully repaired to ensure a smooth foundation.

Professional approach: Our meticulous preparation ensures maximum adhesion and longevity. This critical step is what sets professional refinishing apart from DIY attempts.

STEP 4

Premium Coating Application

Using state-of-the-art spray equipment and eco-friendly, low-VOC coatings, we apply multiple layers to achieve a beautiful, factory-like finish. Our advanced techniques ensure even coverage and exceptional durability.

Quality materials: We use only commercial-grade coatings designed for daily use in high-moisture environments. Your refinished surface will look stunning and last for 10-15 years with proper care.

STEP 5

Curing & Final Inspection

We allow proper curing time and perform a thorough quality inspection. Our technician ensures every detail meets our high standards before considering the job complete.

Our commitment: We clean up completely, leaving your bathroom spotless. You'll receive detailed care instructions and warranty documentation for your peace of mind.

STEP 6

Ready to Use in 24 Hours

After just 24 hours, your beautifully refinished surface is ready for daily use. The coating will continue to harden over the next few days, reaching full durability within a week.
